Toronto Transit Delay Detailed Analysis and Forecasting

The TTC Bus Delay Analytics project transforms over 12 years of raw TTC delay data into an interactive map and dashboard that reveals where, when, and why delays occur across Toronto's transit network. By processing more than 1.3 million records—spanning bus, streetcar, subway, and LRT—the application enables commuters, planners, and advocates to explore delay patterns by route, ward, neighbourhood, and individual stop. Through spatial joins, natural language processing for geocoding, and temporal aggregations, the platform makes complex transit performance data accessible and actionable.
